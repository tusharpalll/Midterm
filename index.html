why cats are better

1. Minimal Maintenance Requirements

Cats exhibit a higher degree of independence compared to dogs. They do not necessitate daily walks or constant supervision, rendering them particularly suitable for individuals with demanding schedules or those who prefer a more relaxed lifestyle.

2. Adaptability to Limited Spaces

Cats are well-suited to smaller living environments, such as apartments. Unlike dogs, they do not require access to a yard for exercise, making them ideal companions for urban dwellers.

3. Natural Grooming Behavior

Cats possess inherent grooming behaviors and devote considerable time to maintaining their cleanliness. This characteristic reduces the burden of hygiene maintenance on their owners.

4. Calm Companionship

Cats are generally quieter than their canine counterparts, making them excellent companions for individuals who value a tranquil atmosphere or who may have sensitivities to noise.
